We make rooftop solar with integrity

SolarSquare is a team of 400+ engineers, MBAs, scientists, creative thinkers driven by the single vision of
accelerating adoption of solar in India. It is an honour to be powering dreams and decarbonising the country.

Pioneering solar excellence:
Our vision in action

Our everyday goal is simple – to make rooftop solar accessible to all. And we do this by focussing on your
happiness, which keeps us constantly innovating.

Our vision is to make clean energy affordable and accessible.

Our mission is to make the solar generation industry more organised

Our method is to infuse transparency and trust.
